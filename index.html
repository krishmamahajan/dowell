Dowell’s Expertise

Termination plays a vital role in power generation, distribution & transmission. Failures are witnessed in end joint due to improper fitment, poor workmanship, lack of knowledge, non- availability of suitable instruments for cable end termination.

Dowell’s with its accumulated experience and constant innovation in terminal technology and their quality standard products have provided wise solution to their end customers.

Dowell's History

The Company was first established in sitabuldi, Nagpur in 1950 by Mr. Patel and started producing Cycle Rickshaws. Mr. Patel with his vast engineering background, subsequently diversified the company into manufacturing: Star-delta Starters in 1954.

Switch gears (introduced for the first time in India by us) in 1958.

Time switches, Centrifugal Switches for Motors, Components of KWH Meters and Push-Button Switches for fans in 1961.

Terminals in 1961.
